# Welcome to Combo Crashout!
### A game by Rahul Murgai. (Biruk was there...)

## Game Overview
Combo Crashout is a fighter game where you can chose to play against a computer or a friend! Using the various controls (instructions available on the starting screen), you can attack your opponent to deplete their health and achieve a knockout. Combos, or preset consecutive moves, can increase your effectiveness and help you win the game. The more combos in a row you get the more effective your attacks are. The player that wins the best of 3 rounds wins the whole game!

## How to Run
**Prerequisites:** You must have a browser with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript compatibility as well as a working internet connection.
**Instructions to run:**
1. [Click here](https://byidnekachew.github.io/combo-crashout/) to open the game. Alternatively, paste this plain text into a new tab in your browser: *https://byidnekachew.github.io/combo-crashout/*
2. Click start to just play or "How To Play" to familiarize yourself with the controls.
